Solution: Kaleidoscobra

Answer: ORANGE PEEL

The puzzle begins with a small line of emojis, a large crossword grid, and a much larger pile of emojis. Either by staring at the grid itself and noticing the colors or starting to look at the crossword clues given by the emojis, solvers should hopefully realize that every color in the grid can be replaced by ROYGBIV as appropriate. For confirmation they're on the right track, the flavor text is intended to clue A British guy fought; didn't win or more traditionally Richard Of York Gave Battle In Vain. From here, solvers can being filling in the grid. To help solvers, the answers to the emoji clues are in alphabetical order.
